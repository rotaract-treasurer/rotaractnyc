## 1. Getting Started

### How to Sign In

1. Go to **[rotaractnyc.org/portal/login](https://rotaractnyc.org/portal/login)**
2. Click **"Sign in with Google"**
3. Choose your Google account (use the email associated with your Rotaract membership)
4. You'll be redirected into the portal

> 💡 **First time?** If you were invited by a board member, your account will be auto-approved. Otherwise, you'll see a "Pending Approval" screen until a board member activates your membership.

## 2. Onboarding Wizard

After your first sign-in, you'll go through a **4-step onboarding** process:

### Step 1: Profile Basics
Fill in your **name**, **phone number**, and **address** (optional).

### Step 2: About You
Tell us about yourself:
- **Short bio** — a sentence or two about you
- **Occupation** & **Employer**
- **Interests** — select from tags like Community Service, Professional Development, etc.
- **Member type** — Professional ($85/year dues) or Student ($65/year dues)

### Step 3: Photo Upload
Upload a **profile photo**. This will appear in the member directory and on your profile card. You can skip this and add one later.

### Step 4: Review & Submit
Review all your information and submit. You'll be taken to a success page, then redirected to your dashboard.

## 4. Events

**Path:** Portal → Events

Browse all club events — service projects, social gatherings, professional development, and more.

### Browsing Events
- **Search** events by name
- **Filter** by type: Free, Paid, Service, Hybrid
- **Toggle** between Upcoming and Past events

### Event Types

| Type | Icon | Description |
|------|------|-------------|
| **Free** | 🎟️ | No cost to attend |
| **Paid** | 💳 | Requires a ticket purchase (member/guest pricing) |
| **Service** | 🤝 | Volunteer/community service event (counts toward service hours) |
| **Hybrid** | 🔄 | Combination event |

### RSVPing to an Event

1. Click on any event to open its **detail page**
2. You'll see the full description, date/time, location, and a map
3. Click one of three RSVP buttons:
   - **Going** ✅ — You're attending
   - **Maybe** 🤔 — You might attend
   - **Not Going** ❌ — You can't make it
4. Your RSVP can be changed anytime before the event

### Paid Events
For paid events, you'll see **ticket tiers** (e.g., Member: $25, Guest: $35). Click **"Get Tickets"** to check out via Stripe. Your RSVP is confirmed once payment succeeds.

### Event Check-In
At the event, a board member may scan a **QR code** from your event page to check you in. This is used for attendance tracking and service hour verification.

### Attendee List
On the event detail page, you can see who else has RSVP'd (Going, Maybe).

## 7. Committees

**Path:** Portal → Committees

### Browsing Committees
Each committee card shows:

1. Click on a committee to view its detail page

### Committee Page
Inside a committee page you can see:
- All current members
- Meeting cadence and term history
- Chair contact information

## 9. Service Hours

**Path:** Portal → Service Hours

Track and log your **community service hours** toward the annual 40-hour goal.

### Logging Hours
1. Click **"Log Hours"**
2. Select the **event** you volunteered at (from a dropdown of past service events)
3. Enter the **number of hours** you served
4. Add an optional **description** of what you did
5. Click **Submit**

### My Hours Tab
View all your submitted hours with:
- Event name
- Hours logged
- Status: **Pending** ⏳ / **Approved** ✅ / **Rejected** ❌
- Reviewer name (once approved/rejected)

### Approval Process
A board member reviews and approves your submitted hours. You'll see the status update in your list.

> 💡 **Tip:** Log your hours within a few days of the event while the details are fresh!

## 11. Dues & Billing

**Path:** Portal → Dues & Billing

Manage your annual membership dues.

### Dues Amounts
| Member Type | Annual Dues |
|-------------|-------------|
| **Professional** | $85/year |
| **Student** | $65/year |

The Rotary year runs **July 1 – June 30**.

### Checking Your Status
The dues page shows your current cycle and payment status:
- **Unpaid** 🔴 — Payment due
- **Paid** 🟢 — You're all set
- **Paid (Offline)** 🟡 — Awaiting treasurer confirmation
- **Waived** ⚪ — Dues waived by the board

### Paying Online (Stripe)
1. Click **"Pay Dues"**
2. You'll be redirected to a secure **Stripe checkout** page
3. Enter your card details and pay
4. Your status updates to **Paid** immediately

### Paying Offline
If you prefer Zelle, Venmo, CashApp, or cash/check:
1. Click **"Pay Offline"**
2. Select your payment method
3. Upload a screenshot/receipt as proof of payment
4. A treasurer will review and approve your payment

> ⚠️ If your dues are unpaid, you'll see a persistent banner at the top of the portal reminding you.

## 14. Forms & Surveys

Sometimes the board will share a **form or survey** for you to fill out — event feedback, interest surveys, volunteer sign-ups, etc.

### How It Works
1. You'll receive a **link** (e.g., `rotaractnyc.org/f/post-gala-survey`)
2. Click the link to open the form
3. Fill in the fields — which may include:
   - Text answers, ratings (⭐), scales (1–10)
   - Dropdowns, checkboxes, radio buttons
   - Date pickers, email, phone inputs
4. Click **Submit**

### Login-Required Forms
Some forms require you to **sign in** before submitting:
- You'll see a 🔒 **Login Required** screen
- Click **"Sign in with Google"**
- Once signed in, your name and email are auto-filled
- A green badge shows "Submitting as **Your Name**"

### Anonymous Forms
Other forms may allow **anonymous** responses — name and email fields are optional.

### Progress Bar
If enabled, a progress bar at the top shows how many fields you've completed.

## 18. Tips & FAQ

### Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: I signed up but see "Pending Approval" — what do I do?**
A: A board member needs to approve your account. This usually happens within 24–48 hours. If it's been longer, email the board at the address on the contact page.

**Q: I used a different Google account — can I switch?**
A: Sign out, then sign back in with the correct Google account. If you need to merge accounts, contact the board.

**Q: How do I RSVP to an event?**
A: Go to Portal → Events, click the event, and select Going/Maybe/Not Going.

**Q: I logged service hours but they show "Pending" — is that normal?**
A: Yes! A board member will review and approve your hours. This is standard procedure.

**Q: Can I pay dues later?**
A: Dues are expected within the grace period of each cycle. A banner will remind you until paid. If you need an extension or financial hardship accommodation, contact the treasurer.

**Q: How do I change my profile photo?**
A: Go to Portal → My Profile, click on your current photo (or the placeholder), and upload a new image.

**Q: The portal isn't loading / I'm getting errors.**
A: Try these steps:
1. Refresh the page (Ctrl/Cmd + R)
2. Clear your browser cache
3. Try a different browser
4. Contact the board if the issue persists

### Keyboard Shortcuts

| Shortcut | Action |
|----------|--------|
| **Cmd/Ctrl + K** | Open search modal |
| **Pull down** (mobile) | Refresh the portal |

### Mobile Experience
The portal is fully responsive — use it on your phone, tablet, or desktop. On mobile:
- The sidebar becomes a **slide-out menu** (tap the ☰ hamburger icon)
- **Pull down** on the main content to refresh
- **Swipe left** on messages to archive
